Description of hourlyweather.prn

The file "hourlyweather.prn" is an input file for VOM, containing hourly weather data in ascii format.

Fields

hour hour Integer, starting with 1, ends with 24
dayyear Day of the year Integer, starting with 1 on 1 January
day Day of the month Integer
month month Integer,starting with 1 on January
year year Integer
tair Air temp (in °C) positive: #.###e±##, negative: -#.###e±##
vd Water vapour deficit (dimensionless, i.e. vd=VPD/P) #.###e±##
parh Photosynthetically active Photon Flux Density (in mol m2 s-1) #.###e±##
rainh Precipitation (in m s-1) #.###e±##
cah ambient air CO2 concentration (in µmol) #.###e±##

The first line contains the variable names and is omitted when reading the file. The first five fields are 8 characters long, the remaining five have 10 characters. The fields need to be filled from behind, e.g.:

       1       1       1       1    2000 3.469e+00 2.119e-03 2.446e-07 0.000e+00 0.350E+03
       2       1       1       1    2000-4.843e-01 1.513e-04 0.000e+00 0.000e+00 0.350E+03

Description of yearly.txt

The file "yearly.txt" is an output file from VOM, containing annual weather and flux data in ascii format for all years of the model run.

Fields

year year
rainyr yearly rainfall (in mm/a)
epanyr None
paryr Photosynthetically active Photon Flux Density (in mol m-2 a-1)
radyr Global solar irradiance (in MJ m-2 a-1)
vdyr Yearly average (of daily averaged) atmospheric vapour deficit (in mol mol-1)
esoilyr Yearly soil evaporation rate (in m a-1)
etyr Yearly transpiration rate by vegetation (in m a-1)
assgyr CO2 uptake rate by grasses (in mol m-2 a-1)
rlgyr Leaf respiration rate by grasses (in mol m-2 a-1)
rrgyr Root respiration rate by grasses (in mol m-2 a-1)
cpccgyr Carbon costs related to water distribution and storage for grasses (in mol m-2 a-1)
tcgyr Carbon costs related to maintenance of gras foliage (in mol m-2 a-1)
asstyr CO2 uptake rate by trees (in mol m-2 a-1)
rltyr Leaf respiration rate by trees (in mol m-2 a-1)
rrtyr Root respiration rate by trees (in mol m-2 a-1)
cpcctyr Carbon costs related to water distribution and storage for trees (in mol m-2 a-1)
tctyr Carbon costs related to maintenance of tree foliage (in mol m-2 a-1)

The first line contains the variable names.

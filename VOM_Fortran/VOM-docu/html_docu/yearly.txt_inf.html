Description of results_yearly.txt

The file "results_yearly.txt" is an output file from VOM, containing annual weather and flux data in ascii format for all years of the model run, e.g.:

 nyear            rain             par            srad              vd           esoil            etmt            etmg            assg             rlg             rrg           cpccg             tcg            etmt            asst             rlt             rrt           cpcct             tct
  1971    0.187668E+04    0.148130E+05    0.712025E+04    0.114804E-01    0.566351E+03    0.000000E+00    0.000000E+00    0.000000E+00   -0.489900E-02    0.286296E+00    0.207360E-02    0.950400E-03    0.000000E+00    0.000000E+00   -0.796850E+01    0.104224E+01    0.348315E+02    0.520344E+01

Fields

nyear Year
rain Annual rainfall (in mm/a)
par Photosynthetically active Photon Flux Density (in mol m-2 a-1)
srad Global solar irradiance (in MJ m-2 a-1)
vd Yearly average (of daily averaged) atmospheric vapour deficit (in mol mol-1)
esoil Yearly soil evaporation rate (in m a-1)
etmt Yearly transpiration rate by vegetation (in m a-1)
etmg Yearly transpiration rate by seasonal vegetation (in m a-1)
assg CO2 uptake rate by seasonal vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
rlg Leaf respiration rate by seasonal vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
rrg Root respiration rate by seasonal vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
cpccg Carbon costs related to water distribution and storage for seasonal vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
tcg Carbon costs related to maintenance of gras foliage (in mol m-2 a-1)
etmt Yearly transpiration rate by perennial vegetation (in m a-1)
asst CO2 uptake rate by perennial vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
rlt Leaf respiration rate by perennial vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
rrt Root respiration rate by perennial vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
cpcct Carbon costs related to water distribution and storage for perennial vegetation (in mol m-2 a-1)
tct Carbon costs related to maintenance of tree foliage (in mol m-2 a-1)

The first line contains the variable names.

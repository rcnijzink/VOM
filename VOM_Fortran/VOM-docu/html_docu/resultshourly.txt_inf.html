Description of resultshourly.txt

The file "results_hourly.txt" is an output file from VOM, containing hourly weather, flux and state variable data in ascii format, e.g.:

 fyear fmonth   fday   nday  nhour           rain           tair            par             vd          esoil             pc        jmax25t        jmax25g            mqt             rl        lambdat        lambdag             rr           asst           assg           etmt           etmg           su_1             zw             ws         spgfcf           infx
  2000      1      1  10586      1    0.31600E-07    0.25800E+02    0.00000E+00    0.55200E-03    0.00000E+00    0.30000E+00    0.50000E-04    0.50000E-04    0.30970E+04   -0.12784E-06    0.28511E+04    0.46179E+04    0.30600E-07    0.00000E+00    0.00000E+00    0.00000E+00    0.00000E+00    0.48785E+00    0.10326E+02    0.50621E+01    0.23958E-04    0.00000E+00

PLEASE TAKE NOTICE OF THE UNITS (some are given per second, others cumulative per hour).

Fields

fyear Year
fmonth Month
fday day of the month
nday Cumulative day of the model run
nhour Hour
rain Rainfall rate (m/s)
tair Air temperature (in oC)
par Photosynthetically active Photon Flux Density (in mol m-2 s-1)
vd Atmospheric vapour deficit (in mol/mol)
esoil Hourly soil evaporation (in m h-2)
pc Projected cover by trees and grasses combined
jmax25t Electron transport capacity by trees (in mol m-2 s-1)
jmax25g Electron transport capacity by grasses (in mol m-2 s-1)
mqt Tissue water content in trees (in kg m-2)
rl Leaf respiration rate by trees and grasses combined (in mol m-2 s-1)
lambdat dE/dA in trees, in mol/mol
lambdag dE/dA in grasses, in mol/mol
rr Root respiration rate by trees and grasses combined (in mol m-2 s-1)
asst Hourly CO2 uptake rate by trees (in mol m-2 h-1)
assg Hourly CO2 uptake rate by grasses (in mol m-2 h-1)
etmt Hourly transpiration rate by trees (in m h-1)
etmg Hourly transpiration rate by grasses (in m h-1)
su_1 Soil saturation degree in the top soil layer (dimensionless)
zw Thickness of the saturated zone (in m)
ws Total soil water storage (in m)
spgfcf Hourly seepage face flow (= subsurface runoff, in m h-1)
infx Hourly infiltration excess runoff (in m h-1)

The first line contains the variable names.

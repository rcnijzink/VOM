Description of resultshourly.txt

The file "resultshourly.txt" is an output file from VOM, containing hourly weather, flux and state variable data in ascii format.

PLEASE TAKE NOTICE OF THE UNITS (SOME OF THEM PER SECOND, OTHERS PER HOUR).

Fields

year year
month month
day day of the month
dcum day of the model run
hour hour
rain m/s
tair Air temp (in oC) of the hour
par Photosynthetically active Photon Flux Density (in mol m-2 s-1)
vd Atmospheric vapour deficit (in mol/mol)
esoil Hourly soil evaporation (in m h-2)
pc Projected cover by trees and grasses combined
jmax25_t Modelled electron transport capacity by trees (in mol m-2 s-1)
jmax25_g Modelled electron transport capacity by grasses (in mol m-2 s-1)
mq Modelled tissue water content in trees (in kg m-2)
rl Leaf respiration rate by trees and grasses combined (in mol m-2 s-1)
lambda_t dE/dA in trees, in mol/mol
lambda_g dE/dA in grasses, in mol/mol
rr Root respiration rate by trees and grasses combined (in mol m-2 s-1)
ass_t Hourly CO2 uptake rate by trees (in mol m-2 h-1)
ass_g Hourly CO2 uptake rate by grasses (in mol m-2 h-1)
het_t Hourly transpiration rate by trees (in m h-1)
het_g Hourly transpiration rate by grasses (in m h-1)
su_1 Soil saturation degree in the top soil layer (dimensionless)
ys Thickness of the saturated zone (in m)
Ws Total soil water storage (in m)
omgo Saturated area fraction
spgfcf Hourly seepage face flow (= subsurface runoff, in m h-1)
infx Hourly infiltration excess runoff (in m h-1)

The first line contains the variable names.

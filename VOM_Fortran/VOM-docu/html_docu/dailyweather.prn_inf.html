Description of dailyweather.prn

The file "dailyweather.prn" is an input file for VOM, containing daily weather data in ascii format.

Fields

Dcum Day of the year Integer, starting with 1 on 1 January
Day Day of the month Integer
Month Month of the year Integer, starting with 1 on January
Year Year Integer, 4 digits
T.Max Daily max. Air temp in °C Decimal, #.##
T.Min Daily min. Air temp in °C Decimal, #.##
Rain Daily rain fall in mm Decimal, #.##
Radn Global solar irradiance in MJ/m2 Decimal, #.##
VP Water vapour pressure in hPa Decimal, #.##
Pres air pressure in hPa Decimal, #.##
Ca ambient air CO2 concentration in µmol Decimal, #.##

The first line contains the variable names and is omitted when reading the file. All fields are 8 characters long and need to be filled from behind, e.g.:

    Dcum     Day   Month    Year   T.Max   T.Min    Rain    Radn      VP    Pres      Ca
       1       1       1    1971   32.87   24.75    0.00   16.44   33.17  999.32  350.00

What is needed?

  • Dcum: used for calculating diurnal radiation
  • Year: used for setting loops
  • T.Max, T.Min: used for calculating diurnal variations in air temperature and VPD
  • Rain: needed for water balance
  • Radn: needed for PAR
  • VP: needed to calculate VPD (needed for calculating hourly VD)
  • Pres: needed for calculating hourly VD
  • Ca: needed to calculate some vegetation variables

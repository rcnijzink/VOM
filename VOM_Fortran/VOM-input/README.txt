Starting from version 56 the meteorological data provided here (dailyweather.prn and hourlyweather.prn) are extracted from a data-set provided by the Land Surface Hydrology Research Group in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Princeton University.

The meteorological data provided here (dailyweather.prn and hourlyweather.prn) are for testing purposes only. To give realistic conditions, the data was taken from CarboEurope IP Continental Integration component (http://www.carboeurope.org/) for a specific flux measurement site.

See the documentation for adjusting model parameters in input.par and shuffle.par.
